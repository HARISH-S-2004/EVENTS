About BIT

Registration Fees:

For IEEE Member : 300/-(without workshop) or 350/-(with workshop) per head.

For Non IEEE Member: 350/-(without workshop) or 400/-(with workshop) per head.


The participant can participate in both technical and non technical events, two technical and one non technical event (or) two technical and one non technical event and one workshop (or) One technical event (or) One Non technical event based on their choice by paying the registration fee once.

The Payment link is attached in the registration form

Cash Prizes and Appreciations certificates will be provided to all the event winners. Certificates will be provided to all the participants.

Accommodation with food will cost Rs.250 per head

The Event Date is April 5&6
